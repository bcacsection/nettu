Mangolian Lamb Balls

Ingredients

  • 00 Gram Lamb (minced)
  • 20 Gram Couscous
  • 20 Gram Scallion
  • 20 Gram Garlic
  • 1 Egg
  • 20 Parsley, chopped
  • For flavour Olive oil
    To garnish Mint
  • 20 Ao nori herb
  • To taste Salt
  • 5 Five spice
  • 5 Cinnamon powder

Directions

1.Add mince lamb, egg , couscous, scallion, parsley, olive oil, five spices and cinnamon powder into a bowl. 2.Mix well and make into balls. 3.Keep the balls into fridge before deep fry. 4.For sauce - add chilly garlic into stock and thick with cornstarch. 5.Add fried meat balls into sauce and simmer until tender. 6.Garnish with mint leaves and serve.
