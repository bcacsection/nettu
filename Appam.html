Appam

Ingredients

1) 1 cup idli rice or sona masuri rice
2) ¼ cup coconut grated
3) ½ tsp sugar
4) ½ tsp salt
5)pinch of baking soda

Directions

  • 1.firstly, grind soaked 1 cup idli rice along with ¼ cup coconut.

  • 2.take 2 tbsp of prepared batter and pour over pan along with ½ cup water. make sure the flame is off.

  • 3. mix well making sure the batter combines well with water.

  • 4.now turn on the stove on medium flame and keep stirring.
    transfer the prepared cooked batter into appam batter.

  • 5.mix well making sure there are no lumps.
    cover and ferment in warm place for 8 hours

  • 6.add in ½ tsp sugar, ½ tsp salt and pinch of baking soda.
    heat appam pan or appachatti over medium flame and pour a ladlefull of batter into the pan.

  • 7.immediately twirl the pan to spread the batter in a circle motion.
    further cover and simmer for about 2 minutes or until the edges turn golden brown and gets cooked completely.

  • 8.12. finally, carefully remove the palappam / appam from the pan and serve with vegetable curry.
